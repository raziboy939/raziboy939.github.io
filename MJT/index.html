Our Story

At My Job Tank, we provide technology driven IT staffing solutions to companies who value top talent. We accomplish this as we broker lasting relationships and delight our clients throughout the staffing process from the moment they approach us until a position is filled.

This commitment to ethical and effective recruiting is rooted in the values of our strategic partner companies in the Wailian Group, and its founder, Linda He. In building Wailian into an international organization that employs 600+ employees specialized in immigration consulting, international investment for Chinese nationals, Linda observed a need for her clients to adapt to their new societies in order to thrive. Accordingly, she has committed herself to community-based projects that continue to put people first.

Linda’s passion for developing each new generation and creating social capital are exemplified by the following projects

Invest in America Summit–In addition to Wailian’s raising of approximately $90 million in capital for local EB-5 immigrant investor projects in the US, for six years running Wailian has organized the Invest in America Summit to promote Chinese investment in the US. This commitment to economic activity continues to benefit both job seekers and employers.

Youth Safe Haven Program–Linda’s investment in this project established by the Eisenhower Foundation supports children aged 6-13 living in American inner city communities. The program provides children access to safe spaces in which to learn, grow, and develop.

Research on Better Immigration Policies, Linda is committed to building and ensuring prosperity in every country that Wailian serves, and she has spearheaded research into ways that immigration policy and investment can be best directed to serve these goals.

Linda’s, Wailian’s, and My Job Tank’s visions share the fundamental principles of helping successive generations of individuals and organizations to grow in order to build employment opportunity, quality of life, and economic growth for all.
