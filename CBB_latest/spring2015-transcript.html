Order your official transcript
To validate your achievement, you must order your official academic transcript BEFORE the pool closes on April 14. This transcript will be automatically sent to us at the end of the Spring quarter by the Office of the University Registrar. Here's how to order your free official transcript:
  1. Login to SISWEB by following the link provided.
  2. Select Student Services & Financial Aid .

  3. Select Student Records .

  4. Select Order Official Transcript. .

  5. Wait for the page to open or click where it says click here . This will take you to a website outside of sisweb.ucdavis.edu.

  6. Once you're on the new page, outside of sisweb, select Start My Order located at the top right of the page.

  7. From here, enter in your personal information as it appears on your transcripts.

  8. You will now be asked to fill out an email address and phone number for the order. Please use your UC Davis Email Address.

  9. On the next page, select Official transcripts-Deliver to recipient followed by Hold until Posting of Current Quarter Grades for and select Spring Term.

  10. On the SAME page, scroll down to Other Information required by University of California, Davis :
    Under Primary reason for Ordering , select For Some Other Reason
    For Attended From Year , type in the year that was your first at Davis.
    For Attended To Year , type in when your last year in Davis will be.
    Further down the page, select Transcript With Work in Progress .

  11. The next screen will ask who the recipient is. Select Other

  12. The next screen asks for the recipient's address. Enter in the following information.

  13. In order to send us your transcripts, select PDF Transcript Downloaded by Third-Party Recipient .
    In Recipient Email Address type in: support@collegebetter.com.

  14. The next screen conrims your address, and the trascriprt format. Your infomation should be listed under Recipient Information , and pdf download should be listed under Type of Recipient Transcript Format. Make sure this is correct and select next.
  15. The next screen will give you an order summary. Confirm that the information is true and select continue.
  16. The last screen gives you an order number. Further down select Finish/Close Window.

  17. Check your UC Davis email address. You will recieve an email from MYSUPPORT@credentialssolutions.com with a reciept of your order. Please forward this email to support@collegebetter.com so we can confirm your order.
